About Us

Passion brewed into every single cup

Welcome to Navanath's Coffee Shop — where we celebrate the rich heritage and exquisite flavours of one of the world's most beloved beverages. Our journey began with a simple yet ambitious dream: to bring the authentic taste of great coffee to lovers everywhere.

Our Story

Navanath's Coffee Shop was founded in 2025 by Navanath Latake, a passionate drink enthusiast with a deep love for traditional Indian coffee culture. Inspired by the diverse regional variations of coffee, we set out to create a dining experience that truly captures the essence of this iconic drink. The youth and young at heart immediately took to the café — it continues to be one of the most happening places in the city.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be a "hangout" spot where people meet, make conversations, and have a whole lot of fun over steaming cups of great coffee. We strive to become the most loved coffee destination in the region — a space where every cup tells a story and every visit creates a memory.

Our Values

  • Authenticity We honour traditional recipes and techniques to deliver a genuine coffee experience.
  • Quality We source the freshest ingredients and finest beans so every sip is flavorful and satisfying.
  • Community We believe in creating a warm, welcoming environment where everyone belongs.
  • Innovation While we cherish tradition, we also embrace innovation to keep our menu exciting and dynamic.

Join Us

We invite you to join us at Navanath's Coffee Shop and embark on a flavourful adventure. Whether you're a lifelong coffee lover or trying it for the first time, we promise a memorable experience that will leave you craving more.

About Us

Welcome to Navanath's Coffee Shop

At Coffee Shop, we celebrate the rich heritage and exquisite flavors of one of the world's most beloved coffees. Our journey began with a simple yet ambitious dream – to bring the authentic taste of Coffee to lovers everywhere.

Our Story

Coffee Shop was founded in 2025 by Navanath Latake, a passionate drink enthusiast with a deep love for traditional Indian cuisine. Inspired by the diverse regional variations of coffees, we set out to create a dining experience that captures the essence of this iconic drink.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be the go-to hangout spot for the youth and the young at heart — a place where people meet, make conversations, and have a great time over steaming cups of great coffee.

Our Values

  • Authenticity: We honor traditional recipes and cooking techniques to deliver a genuine Coffee experience.
  • Quality: We source the freshest ingredients and the finest beans to ensure every sip is flavorful and satisfying.
  • Community: We believe in creating a warm, welcoming environment where everyone can enjoy the shared experience of good coffee.
  • Innovation: While we cherish tradition, we also embrace innovation to keep our menu exciting and dynamic.

Join Us

We invite you to join us at Coffee Shop and embark on a coffee adventure. Whether you're a lifelong fan or trying it for the first time, we promise a memorable experience that will leave you craving more.
